Mock Responsive Petfinder
Website Feature

In Design Interaction Studio I, my team and I created a mockup of an appointment scheduling feature for the Petfinder website. The website is designed to be responsive for desktop and mobile. Our task for this project was to create a website or add-on to a current website to remedy a pain point that users face in the pet adoption process. In order to achieve our final design, our team went through a human-centered design process that involved directed storytelling interviews, scenario creation, think-aloud testing, design pattern implementation, various levels of prototyping, and more.

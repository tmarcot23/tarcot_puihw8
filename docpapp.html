Mock City of Pittsburgh
Department of City Planning App

In Design Interaction Studio II, my team and I created a mockup of an app for the City of Pittsburgh's Department of City Planning. This app's goal is to connect businesses, organizations, and other third-party entities to Pittsburgh residents' ideas for repurposing vacant land. The website is designed to be responsive for desktop and mobile. Our task for this project was to effectively meet the needs of multiple stakeholders in order to achieve the city's goal of repurposing 3000 parcels of vacant land. We created two sides of the app: one for Pittsburgh residents to post ideas and communicate with other app users, and one for Pittsburgh businesses/organizations to view and implement residents' ideas. In order to achieve our final design, our team went through a human-centered design process that involved domain and stakeholder research, a stakeholder map and value flow model, voice and tone creation for the app, various levels of prototyping, and more.
